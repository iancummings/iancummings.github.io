Now that we created your site in your local Dropbox Apps folder, use your favorite HTML / Coding editor to create your site. Save your pages in Dropbox and your site will be updated automatically!

Please remember to visit your site and hit your browser's refresh button every time you make a change in Dropbox. It may take long time to sync your files from Dropbox to duet.to, depending on the size of your site and the number of files.

Use only lowercase letters for folder and file names.
