== 4 The Street is Ours
- just hang on a street corner
- stay connected to what's around you
- connect things that aren't related
  - you put the frame around them and put the together
- look for things that are about to happen, 
  construct the scene in your mind and click!
- your pictures reflect you, your style,
  your artistic identity - be open to your instincts
- stay alert and build the frame
- you can make people move to a direction by
  using your body - you move 
- a simple smile defuses and relaxes people 
  - they are less likely to get grumpy
- intuition, be quick, be happy&excited, be positive
- disadvantaged people; show them but
  don't belittle, shame, etc
