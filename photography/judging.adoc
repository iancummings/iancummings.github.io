== Techniques

=== Paul Hendley

* composition
* sharpness
* exposure

* what's the focus, don't let other parts of the image distract
* interesting places for the eye to rest
* balance!
* horizon

=== not good
* rivers going out of an image at the side
* writing/text in pictures - distracts the eye
* distracting objects

=== good
* people in red coats!
