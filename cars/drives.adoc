== The Ashford triangle / Lambourn Loop
https://se7endrives.wixsite.com/sevendrives/post/the-ashford-triangle---lambourn-loop

1. go to Wantage, at bottom of Chain Hill turn left.
1. Go straight over road, dog-leg Portway, Ickleton Road.
1. At Childrey, turn left to start the triangle

1. start at ///mint.guesswork.points
1. go southwest down B4001 towards Lambourne
1. at Lambourn, turn right B4000
1. at Ashbury, turn right Walnut Trees Hill. B4507
1. at Childrey, turn right Middleway Bottom, sharp right turn to head back to the start
