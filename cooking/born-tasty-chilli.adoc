= Born Tasty Chilli

== Ingredients
Serves: 4, 40mins

* 400g tin mixed beans
* tomato*3
* onion
* corgette
* red pepper
* ? chilli con carne sauce
* ? red chilli
* carrot
* 300g (it can take 500g) beef mince
* rice
* olive oil

* garlic
* 1 tbsp tomato puree
* chilli powder/flakes if not using chilli
* 1/2 tsp cumin
* 1/2 tsp coriander
* 1/2 beef stock cube





== Method

IMPORTANT: use sauteuse/casserole

1. chop onion & carrot - keep together
1. chop corguette, tomatoes, & chilli - keep together

1. add onion & carrot - fry for 2 mins, medium heat
1. brown mince
1. add pepper - fry 3 minutes on high heat
1. add rest of veg - stir fry for 5 minutes on high heat
1. turn down heat, add sauce & beans & stir well
1. add 150ml water, briefly bring to boil and simmer (covered) for 15+ minutes
1. serve with rice or nachos

== Notes
* sauce is tomato puree, onion, garlic, smoked paprika, cumin, oregano, chilli powder

== Method 2 (combination of this and the BBC Good food one)

1. chop *onion* & *carrot* - keep together
1. chop **corguette**, *tomatoes* (& *chilli* if not using spices) - keep together
1. brown **mince**, remove to plate
1. add **onion**, **garlic**, & *carrot* - fry for 2 mins, medium heat
1. fry *tomato puree* for 2 mins
1. *spices* (cumin, coriander, chilli powder)
1. add *pepper* - fry 3 minutes on high heat
1. add rest of *veg* - stir fry for 5 minutes on high heat
1. turn down heat, return **mince**, add *sauce* & *beans* & stir well
1. add 150ml wine, briefly bring to boil and simmer (covered) for 15+ minutes
